# Team Bunker Romeo – WRO Future Engineers 2026

We are a team from Baja California, México, composed of two members: Ian Fernando Rivera Armenta and Jacobo Arteaga Castañeda from Bunker Robotics. Both team members have experience in other competitions, specifically the Robomission category, totaling 9 years of combined experience.

This is our second year in the Future Engineers category. We began our preparation at the start of the year (January 2025), and over time, our robot has gone through several iterations.
